How to Play MindTrap

Welcome to MindTrap — a logic and puzzle-solving game designed to challenge your mind and test your creativity. Here’s how you can play:

  • 🧩 Each level presents a unique puzzle or riddle.
  • 🕒 Solve it within the given time limit to score points.
  • 💡 Think logically — the clues may be tricky!
  • 🏆 The faster you solve, the higher your score.
  • 🎯 Advance through levels and test your problem-solving skills.
